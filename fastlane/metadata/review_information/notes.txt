imanage is a fully offline photo file organizer. No account or login is required.

How to test:
1. Launch the app.
2. Click "Choose Files…" (or drop photo files / a folder onto the window) and pick any JPEG photos that contain camera EXIF.
3. A preview tree of the destination folders appears. Click the Organize button to move the files.
4. Cmd-Z undoes the operation and restores every file to its original location.

The app never modifies photo contents and never sends any data over the network.
